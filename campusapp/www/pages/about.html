    General information

    Who wants to be responsible

    Information about individual processing of requests

    Processing of log data (access data): When using the website, access to pages, whether the access was successful, the point of time, the volume of data and the IP address of the requesting computer can raise errors. The storage of the IP address is shortened, so that an identification, which stands no way for the gain of knowledge of the requesting connection, is not possible or only possible at a cost. The processing can only be internal and on basis of Art. 6 para. 1 f) of the EU GDPR, whereby the legitimate interest lies in error detection.

    The saved log data will be deleted manually after an indefinite time.

    Collection and processing of personal data

    Sensitive and personal data are stored permanently only on the respective terminal of the user. Personal data within the meaning of data protection regulations are individual details concerning the personal or factual circumstances as well as the user’s behavior. The user has the option of immediately, completely and permanently removing all recorded data in the settings of the application. After removal, the data can be re-entered by entering.

    Cookies

    The platform uses cookies, which help make the SAM in One website more user-friendly, efficient, and secure. Cookies are small text files that are stored on a user’s computer and saved by the browser. Most of the cookies the platform uses are so-called "session cookies." They are automatically deleted after a user’s visit. Other cookies remain in the device's memory until users delete them. These cookies make it possible to recognize the user’s browser when the user next visits the site. Without cookies, for example if they have been deactivated in the browser, full use of this application is not possible.

  • FAQ
    • What is SAM in One?

      SAM in One is a universal app for gathering information, and to summarize important service portals of the universities. The main users are students and staff at the Universities.

    • Is SAM in One free of charge?

      Yes, SAM in One is free of charge and will always remain so.

    • Can I use SAM in One at my university?

      SAM in One is currently set up for the University of Heilbronn and will be completed and tested there. After that it will be developed for more Universities!
